On the Subject of A Word From Our Sponsors

If only I knew who was actually getting the money from these things.

When the module activates, scroll through the available advertisements and press the round button to choose which one to watch before the timer runs out.

Technical issues may result in the advertisement being louder than other game audio. We apologise for any inconvenience.

For contractual reasons, all advertisements must be played before any can be repeated. Choosing a repeat when it is not allowed will still cause the ad to play, but at the cost of a strike.
